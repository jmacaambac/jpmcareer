Corporate Security Assistant Manager

As a Corporate Security Assistant Manager, I played a critical role in protecting Aseana Real Estate’s extensive properties by implementing comprehensive security measures, traffic management solutions, and risk mitigation strategies. Working closely with the Corporate Security Manager, I ensured that security operations, emergency protocols, and property protection strategies were effectively executed across all company-owned estates. My role encompassed policy enforcement, crisis response, traffic flow management, and strategic security planning to create a safe and secure environment for residents, tenants, and visitors.

Security Management & Risk Mitigation

*Policy Enforcement & Compliance: Assisted in enforcing and monitoring the company’s security rules and regulations, ensuring strict compliance by tenants, visitors, employees, and third-party service providers.

*Security Planning for New Developments: Designed and implemented security measures for newly acquired estates and properties, integrating access control systems, perimeter security, and surveillance technology to mitigate potential risks.

*Risk Assessment & Threat Mitigation: Conducted risk analysis to identify vulnerabilities in real estate assets and provided security recommendations to prevent theft, vandalism, and unauthorized access.

*Incident Investigation & Emergency Response: Led investigations on security breaches, property damages, and internal violations, providing detailed reports with preventive solutions. Coordinated with law enforcement during major security incidents.

*Security Force Supervision & Deployment: Managed the deployment of security personnel, ensuring optimized manpower distribution, proper training, and adherence to protocols across various properties.

Traffic & Crowd Control Management

*Traffic Flow & Congestion Mitigation: Developed and implemented traffic management plans to ensure smooth vehicle and pedestrian flow within the estates. Coordinated with local traffic authorities to regulate congestion during peak hours and special events.

*Traffic Enforcement & Road Safety Compliance: Monitored and enforced traffic rules and parking regulations, ensuring compliance with government-mandated road safety protocols. Addressed illegal parking, unauthorized road obstructions, and reckless driving to maintain order within the premises.

*Event Traffic & Crowd Control: Designed crowd control measures for high-footfall areas, VIP movements, and major estate events to ensure safety and minimize disruptions. Deployed security teams and traffic marshals to maintain smooth operations.

*Coordination with Local Transport Authorities: Worked with government agencies and private transport services to enhance public transportation accessibility and regulate traffic conditions around the estate.

Surveillance & Access Control

*CCTV Monitoring & Security System Oversight: Ensured that CCTV networks, access control points, and perimeter security systems were fully operational and effective in preventing security incidents. Conducted regular system audits to identify areas for improvement.

*Visitor & Contractor Access Supervision: Implemented stricter access control policies for employees, visitors, and contractors, ensuring that only authorized personnel could enter restricted areas. Strengthened ID verification, vehicle inspection, and baggage screening processes.

Inter-Agency & Stakeholder Coordination

*Law Enforcement & Emergency Response Partnerships: Maintained direct coordination with law enforcement, fire departments, and medical emergency teams to ensure quick response to security threats and emergencies.

*Tenant & Community Engagement: Worked closely with residential and commercial tenants to address security concerns, promote crime prevention awareness, and ensure adherence to estate regulations.

Security Budget & Resource Allocation

*Cost-Effective Security Management: Assisted in budgeting for security operations, equipment upgrades, and personnel training, ensuring the efficient allocation of resources to maximize safety without exceeding financial limits.

Through my expertise in security operations, risk assessment, traffic management, and crisis response, I helped enhance the overall safety, efficiency, and security resilience of Aseana Real Estate’s properties. My leadership ensured a well-organized security framework, improved traffic flow, and a protected environment for all stakeholders.
