Operations Manager

As Operations Manager at Detectnet Security Agency Corporation, I was responsible for overseeing the day-to-day security operations and ensuring the protection of company assets, personnel, and clients. My role focused on developing security policies, optimizing resource utilization, managing emergency response strategies, and enhancing operational efficiency. By implementing proactive security measures and workforce training, I strengthened the agency’s ability to mitigate risks, prevent security breaches, and respond effectively to incidents.

Security Operations & Asset Protection

Developed and enforced comprehensive security policies to protect physical assets from theft, vandalism, and unauthorized access.

Implemented emergency response procedures and crisis management plans to handle fire hazards, break-ins, workplace violence, and other security threats.

Led investigations on security incidents, working closely with law enforcement agencies to provide necessary support and evidence.

Oversaw deployment of security personnel across multiple locations, ensuring proper staffing, scheduling, and performance evaluation.

Operational Risk Assessment & Threat Mitigation

Conducted risk assessments and security audits to evaluate the vulnerability of company facilities, identifying and addressing potential security gaps.

Designed and implemented access control measures, including ID verification, visitor management protocols, and restricted access policies.

Established supplier delivery security protocols, ensuring all inbound deliveries were made at a single, guarded location to minimize security breaches and unauthorized access.

Security Awareness & Training Programs

Developed security awareness programs to educate employees on best practices, threat identification, and emergency response.

Trained staff on loss prevention strategies, emphasizing the importance of immediately reporting lost access cards, suspicious activities, and security breaches.

Conducted drills and training sessions for security personnel to improve situational awareness and response efficiency.

Vendor & Equipment Management

Maintained strong professional relationships with manufacturers and suppliers of security equipment, ensuring access to the latest security technologies.

Managed the procurement and maintenance of security systems, including CCTV cameras, alarm systems, access control devices, and intrusion detection technologies.

Ensured that all security equipment met industry standards, conducting regular functionality checks and upgrades when necessary.

Budget & Resource Optimization

Managed the security budget, ensuring cost-effective allocation of resources while maintaining high-security standards.

Optimized personnel utilization, balancing security coverage with operational costs to enhance efficiency.

Implemented cost-saving measures, such as contract renegotiations and improved scheduling, without compromising security effectiveness.

Incident Management & Law Enforcement Coordination

Provided support to law enforcement agencies during investigations, supplying critical information, reports, and surveillance footage.

Established collaborative partnerships with police, fire departments, and emergency response teams to enhance security preparedness and response coordination.

Developed a Mobile Incident Report Response Form that would immediately deliver the details ahead of time to the operations team and come with an urgent temporary and or permanent resolution

Led post-incident analysis and corrective action planning, ensuring continuous improvement in security operations.

Key Achievements & Impact:

Successfully eliminated major security vulnerabilities through updated policies and stricter access control procedures.

Strengthened workforce security awareness, reducing incidents of unauthorized access and internal theft.

Enhanced security vendor relationships, ensuring cost-effective procurement and maintenance of security equipment.

Improved incident response time through better training, strategic resource allocation, and stronger law enforcement partnerships.

As Operations Manager, I played a pivotal role in enhancing security resilience, streamlining operational processes, and strengthening risk management within Detectnet Security Agency Corporation. My leadership in policy development, training programs, and security technology integration significantly improved the agency’s ability to protect assets, personnel, and clients across multiple sites.

Recognized for my significant contributions to the company, I was promoted to General Manager of Detectnet Security Agency Corporation. My leadership and strategic approach played a pivotal role in expanding the agency’s client base and increasing the deployment of highly trained security personnel across various locations. Through my strong business development efforts, operational efficiency strategies, and commitment to service excellence, I successfully secured new contracts with reputable clients, strengthening the company’s reputation in the security industry. Additionally, I implemented improved security protocols, workforce training programs, and client-focused solutions, ensuring that both existing and new clients received top-tier security services tailored to their needs. My tenure as General Manager was marked by substantial business growth, enhanced operational performance, and a more structured and professionalized security workforce.
