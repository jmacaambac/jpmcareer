Security and Loss Prevention Operations Supervisor

As a Security and Loss Prevention Operations Supervisor at Entrego Express Corporation, I played a key role in ensuring the security, safety, and operational efficiency of the company’s logistics network. My responsibilities encompassed policy enforcement, security operations, risk mitigation, and fraud prevention, all aimed at protecting company assets, personnel, and parcels across regional hubs.

Policy Implementation & Compliance: Assisted the Associate Manager in enforcing security policies, ensuring strict adherence to protocols that safeguarded company resources and minimized risks.

Regional Operations Oversight: Supervised security and production operations across multiple regional hubs, ensuring seamless coordination between security personnel and operational teams.

CCTV Management & Monitoring: Oversaw the nationwide CCTV infrastructure, ensuring all surveillance systems were fully functional. Promptly identified and reported malfunctions to minimize security vulnerabilities.

Security & Safety Audits: Conducted quarterly Security and Safety Assessments and Vulnerability Audits to identify weaknesses and implement corrective measures, enhancing overall risk management.

Personnel Oversight & Payroll Accuracy: Verified Daily Time Records (DTRs) of security personnel to ensure compliance with the reverse billing policy, preventing discrepancies in security service expenses..

Incident Investigation & Fraud Prevention: Led investigations into security breaches, theft, and other incidents, providing actionable reports and preventive recommendations. Proactively monitored for fraudulent activities, taking immediate corrective action when necessary.

Rider Vetting & Background Checks: Conducted comprehensive vetting procedures for newly hired, active, and resigning riders, ensuring that only qualified and trustworthy personnel were part of the logistics network.

Parcel Accountability & Loss Prevention: Implemented strict monitoring measures to ensure that all parcels were properly accounted for, reducing the risk of theft, misplacement, or loss in transit.

Special Investigative Assignments & Management Directives: Took on high-priority investigative activities as assigned by management, delivering detailed reports and actionable insights to support decision-making.

Through my proactive leadership and strategic approach, I contributed to strengthening Entrego’s loss prevention framework, improving security efficiency, and minimizing risks associated with fraudulent activities, operational lapses, and logistics security.
