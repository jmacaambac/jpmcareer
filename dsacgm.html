General Manager

As General Manager of Detectnet Security Agency Corporation, I was responsible for the overall leadership, strategic planning, and operational management of the organization. My role involved directing security operations, driving business growth, ensuring financial sustainability, and overseeing the performance of various departments, including human resources, client relations, marketing, and financial management. I worked closely with the Board of Directors to establish both short-term and long-term business strategies that enhanced service quality, operational efficiency, and market competitiveness.

Strategic Leadership & Business Development

Provided visionary leadership by assisting the Board of Directors and executive staff in developing long-range and annual business plans that aligned with market trends and industry best practices.

Conducted in-depth market research, competitive analysis, and strategic planning to identify business expansion opportunities and enhance the company's service portfolio.

Spearheaded the development and implementation of company policies and operational guidelines to maintain a high standard of security services and workforce performance.

Operations & Security Management

Oversaw the entire security operations of the agency, ensuring the effective deployment, training, and supervision of security personnel across various client sites.

Developed standard operating procedures (SOPs) and emergency response protocols to enhance incident management, risk mitigation, and threat response strategies.

Led investigations and audits to assess security vulnerabilities, address breaches, and implement corrective measures.

Human Resource Management & Workforce Development

Managed recruitment, training, and career development programs to maintain a highly skilled and professional security workforce.

Established performance evaluation systems, incentive programs, and disciplinary measures to enhance employee engagement, productivity, and accountability.

Fostered a culture of professionalism, teamwork, and ethical conduct within the organization.

Marketing & Client Relations

Acted as the primary representative of the company in building and maintaining relationships with key clients, government agencies, and industry partners.

Led business development and sales initiatives, securing new contracts and expanding the company's market presence.

Negotiated service agreements, client contracts, and strategic partnerships to drive revenue growth and operational expansion.

Developed marketing campaigns and promotional materials to enhance brand visibility and attract new clients.

Financial Management & Budget Oversight

Oversaw the financial planning, budgeting, and resource allocation to ensure the company’s profitability and financial stability.

Developed cost-effective strategies to maximize revenue and minimize operational expenses while maintaining service excellence.

Ensured compliance with financial reporting requirements, tax obligations, and audit processes.

Administrative & Compliance Management

Ensured the agency’s full compliance with local laws, industry regulations, and licensing requirements governing security service providers.

Supervised the preparation of the company’s Annual Report, summarizing progress on financial performance, operational achievements, and strategic goals.

Authored business proposals, policy papers, and analytical reports to support organizational decision-making and long-term planning.

Key Achievements & Impact:

Successfully expanded the company’s client base, securing high-value contracts and increasing revenue streams.

Enhanced operational efficiency by implementing innovative security solutions and workforce optimization strategies.

Strengthened client satisfaction and retention by ensuring top-tier security services and responsive customer support.

Developed a high-performing management team, fostering leadership skills and succession planning within the organization.

Through visionary leadership, operational excellence, and strategic business management, I played a critical role in driving the growth, stability, and success of Detectnet Security Agency Corporation. My ability to streamline operations, improve financial performance, and elevate service quality positioned the company as a trusted provider of security solutions all throughout the Philippines.
